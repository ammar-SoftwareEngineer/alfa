Project overview

Project E57 is located in New Narcissus at the heart of Fifth Settlement. It features a premium corner location with open views and direct access to major New Cairo axes. The project includes elegant facades, hotel-style entrances, electronic parking, 24/7 security, video intercom, electric elevators, CCTV systems, and armored doors. Unit areas range from 147 sqm to 294 sqm with practical internal layouts designed for modern family living.

  • 24/7 Security: Gated access and on-site security for residents and visitors.
  • Electric elevators: Smooth vertical circulation across all residential levels.
  • Armored doors: Added protection and peace of mind at unit entrances.
  • Hotel-style parking: Structured parking with clear circulation and lighting.
  • CCTV monitoring: Coverage across key common areas for safer living.
  • Smart space planning: Efficient layouts from 147 sqm to 294 sqm for family use.

Why this project

Alfa combines prime addressing with dependable execution: classic facades, practical layouts, and integrated building services. E57 reflects that approach—strong street presence, controlled common areas, and units sized for real family needs in one of New Cairo’s most active corridors.
